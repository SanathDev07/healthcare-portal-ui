Redesigning the healthcare member portal experience — from confusing insurance jargon to a calm, intelligent, AI-powered self-service platform.

The Problem

Healthcare portals fail their members daily.

60% of members call support for questions the portal should answer. Insurance jargon creates anxiety. Claims filing takes 8+ minutes and 40% of users abandon. Members don't know what their plan covers until they get a surprise bill.

Design

Calm, clear, and intelligent.

Five core screens designed to reduce member anxiety and surface the right information at the right moment.

Member Dashboard

Health stats, deductible progress, upcoming appointments and smart alerts — all above the fold.

AI Benefits Copilot

Conversational AI with streaming responses, confidence indicators, and always-visible human escalation.

Appointments

Upcoming and past visits with one-tap joining for virtual appointments and inline rescheduling.

Benefits Navigator

Visual deductible tracking, coverage details in plain language, and expandable accordion per benefit type.

Key UX Decisions
🤖

AI Copilot with Trust Architecture

Designed confidence indicators, source citations ("Based on your 2024 Cigna PPO plan"), and a persistent "Talk to a human" button — because healthcare AI needs to earn trust, not assume it.

📋

Plain Language Over Insurance Jargon

Every insurance term replaced with plain English and tooltips. "EOB" became "Here's what was covered and what you owe." Validated with Maria persona in 3 rounds of usability testing.

♿

Accessibility-First — WCAG 2.1 AA

All text at 4.5:1+ contrast ratio. Skip links, ARIA live regions for AI streaming, keyboard navigation, 44px minimum touch targets. Designed for Eleanor — works for everyone.

📱

Mobile-First Dashboard

Maria uses mobile 70% of the time. Dashboard prioritized single most-important action per section. Progressive disclosure kept the most complex information below the fold.

WCAG 2.1 AA

94% compliance achieved after 4 rounds of audit and remediation — up from 76% baseline. All color ratios verified, keyboard navigation fully functional, AI streaming announced to screen readers.
