# User Journey Map — Filing a Health Insurance Claim

## Scenario
Maria (Persona 1) visits an urgent care clinic and needs to file an out-of-network claim for reimbursement.

---

## Journey Stages

### Stage 1 — Trigger (Day 0)
**Action:** Maria visits urgent care. Receives itemized bill for $380.  
**Thought:** "Will my insurance cover this? How do I get reimbursed?"  
**Emotion:** 😰 Anxious  
**Pain Points:** 
- Doesn't know if the clinic was in-network
- Bill has medical codes she doesn't understand
**Opportunity:** Proactive post-visit notification with coverage estimate

---

### Stage 2 — Discovery (Day 1-2)
**Action:** Searches "how to file a Cigna claim" — lands on generic FAQ page.  
**Thought:** "This is so confusing. Do I need a form? Where do I upload the bill?"  
**Emotion:** 😤 Frustrated  
**Pain Points:**
- 4 clicks to find the claim form
- Form terminology is legal/medical jargon
**Opportunity:** AI copilot surfaces claim flow proactively after visit detection

---

### Stage 3 — Submission (Day 3)
**Action:** Finds claim form. Uploads bill. Fills out 14-field form.  
**Thought:** "Did it go through? I have no idea what happens next."  
**Emotion:** 😐 Uncertain  
**Pain Points:**
- No real-time validation — form errors only shown on submit
- No estimated processing time given
- No confirmation email for 2 hours
**Opportunity:** Progressive form with inline validation + instant confirmation + clear timeline

---

### Stage 4 — Waiting (Day 4-12)
**Action:** Checks portal daily. No status updates visible.  
**Thought:** "Did they lose my claim? Should I call?"  
**Emotion:** 😤 Frustrated → calls member services (30 min hold)  
**Pain Points:**
- Claim status tracker doesn't exist
- Calls overwhelm member services team
**Opportunity:** Real-time claim status tracker with push notifications

---

### Stage 5 — Resolution (Day 14)
**Action:** Receives EOB by mail. Reimbursement check arrives Day 18.  
**Thought:** "Finally. But I still don't fully understand what they covered."  
**Emotion:** 😌 Relieved but confused  
**Pain Points:**
- EOB is a paper document with confusing format
- No digital breakdown of what was covered vs denied
**Opportunity:** Digital EOB with plain-language explanation and AI Q&A

---

## Key Insights
1. **Biggest drop-off:** Between discovery and submission (43% of users abandon)
2. **Highest anxiety:** Waiting stage — no visibility into claim status
3. **Most requested feature:** Real-time claim tracker (89% of survey respondents)
4. **AI opportunity:** Copilot can answer "what does this EOB mean?" reducing call volume by est. 24%

---

## Redesign Priorities (ranked)
1. 🔴 Claim status real-time tracker
2. 🔴 Simplified 5-field claim submission form  
3. 🟡 AI-powered EOB plain-language explainer
4. 🟡 Proactive post-visit claim reminder notification
5. 🟢 Digital EOB with itemized breakdown
